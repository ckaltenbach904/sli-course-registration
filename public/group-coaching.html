Group Coaching Program

Our Group Coaching programs are designed to help you develop the essential skills you need to lead teams and drive success. Through interactive workshops and expert coaching, you will be equipped with the tools to transform your leadership capabilities.

The course is available as both self-paced and online live classes, allowing you to choose the format that best suits your schedule and learning style. No matter which format you choose, the cost of the Group Couaching program is $299 per person. The GC Program is limited up to 5-6 people per class.
