Agile Mindset and 7 Covey Habits

Developing an Agile Mindset is essential for thriving in the modern, fast-paced business environment. The Agile Mindset focuses on flexibility, adaptability, and continuous improvement, enabling leaders and teams to respond to changes quickly and effectively.

Our Agile Mindset program combines principles of agile methodologies, which emphasize iterative development, customer collaboration, and responsiveness, with the renowned teachings from Stephen Covey’s 7 Habits of Highly Effective People. This blend of leadership philosophies fosters a holistic approach to leading teams and organizations toward success.

The course is available as both self-paced and online live classes, allowing you to choose the format that best suits your schedule and learning style. No matter which format you choose, the cost of the Agile Mindset program is $799 per person.

Key Benefits of Developing an Agile Mindset:

  • Adaptability: Be prepared to quickly pivot in response to change.
  • Continuous Improvement: Always strive to improve processes and solutions.
  • Team Empowerment: Foster a collaborative environment where teams have the autonomy to innovate and solve problems.
  • Customer Focus: Keep the customer at the center of all decisions and adapt based on feedback.
  • Efficient Decision-Making: Shorten decision-making cycles to stay ahead of the competition.
