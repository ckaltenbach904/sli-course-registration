Introduction

At the Servant-Leader Institut (SLI), we are committed to developing the next generation of servant leaders who prioritize the well-being of their teams and communities. We believe in the transformative power of servant leadership, where empathy, collaboration, and vision create sustainable growth for individuals and organizations.

Our mission is to empower leaders with cutting-edge tools, strategies, and techniques that emphasize agile coaching, communication excellence, and management innovation. Our programs are designed to bridge traditional leadership principles with modern methodologies, fostering a culture of trust, transparency, and shared success.

Whether you are an aspiring leader or a seasoned professional, SLI offers exclusive training programs, hands-on group coaching, and recognized certifications to equip you with the skills needed to lead in today’s dynamic business world.

About Us

The Servant-Leader Institut (SLI) is a startup with a mission to empower servant leaders with agile coaching, communication, and management principles, tools, and techniques. We offer exclusive training programs, leadership development, and certifications to enhance your leadership skills.

SLI focuses on combining servant leadership with agile methodologies to bring about positive change in individuals and organizations. Our trainers and coaches bring decades of experience across diverse industries, and we pride ourselves on fostering a community of continuous learners who aim to make a meaningful impact.

Certification Preparation

We offer certification preparation for a variety of Project Management Institute (PMI) certifications. These certifications are globally recognized and help demonstrate your proficiency in project management and leadership.


Project Management Professional (PMP)

The PMP certification is the gold standard for project management. It demonstrates your expertise in managing teams, projects, and workflows. Learn more about PMP

Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)

The CAPM certification is perfect for those looking to start their project management career, providing foundational knowledge and skills. Learn more about CAPM

Agile Certified Practitioner (ACP)

The PMI-ACP certification validates your expertise in Agile principles and practices, helping you lead Agile teams more effectively. Learn more about ACP

Professional in Business Analysis (PBA)

The PMI-PBA certification demonstrates your ability to perform business analysis tasks, identify business needs, and recommend solutions. Learn more about PBA

Risk Management Professional (RMP)

The PMI-RMP certification proves your ability to assess and mitigate project risks, helping ensure successful project outcomes. Learn more about RMP

Program Management Professional (PgMP)

The PgMP certification highlights your ability to manage multiple, related projects and ensure alignment with organizational goals. Learn more about PgMP
